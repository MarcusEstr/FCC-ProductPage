What is City Pop?

"City pop referred to the likes of Sugar Babe and Eiichi Ohtaki, artists who scrubbed out the Japanese influences of their predecessors and introduced the sounds of jazz and R&B — genres said to have an 'urban' feel — to their music... The term has drifted in and out of the musical lexicon ever since..." - Ryotaro Aoki, The Japan Times
